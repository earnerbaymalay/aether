# 🌌 A E T H E R — U S A G E
### *The Definitive User Journey & Onboarding Guide*

Welcome to the **Aether** ecosystem. This document is your technical manual for navigating the **Neural Operating Interface**. 

---

## 🛠 Step 1: Initialization (The Deploy)
The Aether deployment is a one-command experience.

1. **Clone & Execute**:
```bash
git clone https://github.com/earnerbaymalay/aether.git
cd aether && ./install.sh
```

2. **The Shortcut**: Type **`ai`** from anywhere in your Termux terminal to launch the interface.

---

## 🚀 Step 2: The Neural Launchpad
When you type `ai`, you'll enter the **Neural Orchestrator**.

- **Select a Pathway**: Choose the model best suited for your task (Turbo, Agent, Logic, Code).
- **System Health**: Monitor battery, storage, and vault status directly from the header.
- **Boot Sequence**: Aether performs real-time system checks on every launch.

---

## 🤖 Step 3: Operating the Agent
The **🤖 AGENT** (Hermes-8B) is your primary collaborator. 

- **Tool Execution**: Ask it to "List files in the current directory" or "Scan for security issues."
- **Knowledge Acquisition**: Provide a technical insight and say "Learn this: [File]| [Content]."
- **File Manipulation**: The Agent can read and write code, debug scripts, and manage your workspace.

---

## 🛠 Step 4: Advanced Tools & Swarms
Aether isn't limited to a single model. Use the **TOOLS** menu for advanced orchestration:

- **🐞 Debug Console**: Real-time log monitoring and **Self-Healing** capabilities.
- **🛒 Skill Marketplace**: Install or develop new local neural capabilities.
- **🧠 Swarm Orchestrator**: Initiate multi-agent missions for complex architectural planning.
- **📖 Librarian**: Perform automated audits and deduplication of your Context7 vault.

---

## 💾 Step 5: Context7 & Obsidian
Your learned knowledge is stored in `~/aether/knowledge/context7/`.

1. **Sync with Obsidian**: Open the `aether/` folder as a vault in the Obsidian Android app.
2. **Visualize Growth**: Watch your neural network grow as the AI archives technical breakthroughs.
3. **Local First**: All data is Markdown, accessible and readable by any text editor.

---

## 🔧 Maintenance & Updates
Keep your neural interface at peak performance:
- **`./install.sh`**: Re-run to update dependencies or rebuild the engine.
- **`ai` -> `TOOLS` -> `🧹 PURGE`**: Reset the current session context window.
- **`./bench.sh`**: Re-profile your hardware for optimal performance.
